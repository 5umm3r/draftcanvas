Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Draft Canvas collects the following information:

  • Purchase information: When you make a purchase, Polar collects your email address and payment details. We do not directly store payment card information.
  • License information: When activating your license, we collect a device identifier (hashed Mac UUID).
  • Crash reports: If the App crashes, information may be sent to Apple via macOS's standard error reporting mechanism.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify license validity and manage device activations
  • To respond to purchase and support inquiries
  • To improve the App

3. Third-Party Services

We may share collected information with the following service providers:

  • Polar Software Inc. (Stripe): Payment processing and license management

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except as required by law.

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect aggregated page view data. This tool does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. For details, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

License information is stored securely in your Mac's Keychain. Purchase information is stored on Polar's servers.

5. Your Rights

To access or delete your personal information, contact us at support@spade3.com.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@spade3.com.
